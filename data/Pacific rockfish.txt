the scientific name of pacific rockfish is sebastes. sebastes is a genus of fish in the family sebastidae (though some include this in scorpaenidae), most of which have the common name of rockfish. a few are called ocean perch, sea perch or redfish instead. most of the sebastes species live in the north pacific, although two (s. capensis and s. oculatus) live in the south pacific/atlantic and four (s. fasciatus, s. mentella, s. norvegicus and s. viviparus) live in the north atlantic. the coast off south california is the area of highest rockfish diversity, with 56 species living in the southern california bight.
