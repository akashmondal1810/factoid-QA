the scientific name of striped bass is morone saxatilis. the striped bass (morone saxatilis), also called atlantic striped bass, striper, linesider, rock, pimpfish, or rockfish, is the state fish of maryland, rhode island, south carolina, and the state saltwater (marine) fish of new york, new jersey, virginia, and new hampshire. they are also found in the minas basin and gaspereau river in nova scotia, canada.
