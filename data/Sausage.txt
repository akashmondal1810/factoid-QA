sausage is a food usually made from ground meat (normally pork or beef), mixed with salt, herbs, and other spices, although vegetarian sausages are available. typically, a sausage is formed in a casing traditionally made from intestine, but sometimes synthetic. some sausages are cooked during processing and the casing may be removed afterwards. sausage making is a traditional food preservation technique. sausages may be preserved by curing, drying, or smoking.
