a baked potato, or jacket potato, is the edible result of baking a potato. when well cooked, a baked potato has a fluffy interior and a crisp skin. it may be served with fillings and condiments such as butter, cheese or ham. potatoes can be baked in a conventional gas or electric oven, a convection oven, a microwave oven, on a barbecue grill, or on/in an open fire. some restaurants use special ovens designed specifically to cook large numbers of potatoes then keep them warm and ready for service.
