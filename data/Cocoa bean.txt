the scientific name of cocoa bean is theobroma cacao. cocoa bean (also cacao bean, often simply cocoa and cacao; mayan: kakaw; nahuatl: cacaua) is the dried and fully fermented fatty seed of theobroma cacao, from which cocoa solids and cocoa butter are extracted. they are the basis of chocolate, as well as many mesoamerican foods such as mole sauce and tejate.
