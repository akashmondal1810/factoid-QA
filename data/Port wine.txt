port wine (also known as vinho do porto, portuguese pronunciation: [?vi?udu?po?tu], porto, and usually simply port) is a portuguese fortified wine produced exclusively in the douro valley in the northern provinces of portugal. it is typically a sweet, red wine, often served as a dessert wine though it also comes in dry, semi-dry, and white varieties. fortified wines in the style of port are also produced outside portugal, most notably in australia, south africa, canada, india, argentina, and the united states.
