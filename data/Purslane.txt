the scientific name of purslane is portulaca oleracea. purslane is a common name for several plants with edible leaves and may refer to: portulacaceae, a family of succulent flowering plants, and especially: portulaca oleracea, a species of portulaca eaten as a vegetable and considered a weed, known as summer purslane portulaca grandiflora, moss rose, or moss-rose purslane claytonia perfoliata, miner's lettuce or winter purslane claytonia sibirica, pink purslane halimione portulacoides, sea purslane
