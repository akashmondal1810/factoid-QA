the scientific name of yellowtail amberjack is seriola lalandi. the yellowtail amberjack or great amberjack, seriola lalandi, buri or hiramasa is a large fish found in the pacific and indian oceans. it can be divided into three subspecies: the california yellowtail, seriola lalandi dorsalis the southern yellowtail, or in new zealand and australia the yellowtail kingfish or kingfish s. l. lalandi the asian yellowtail, s. l. aureovitta.
