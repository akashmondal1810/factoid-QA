the scientific name of common dab is limanda limanda. the common dab, limanda limanda, is an edible flatfish of the family pleuronectidae. it is a demersal fish native to shallow seas around northern europe, in particular the north sea, where it lives on sandy bottoms down to depths of about 100 metres . it can reach 40 centimetres in length and can weigh up to 1 kilogram, though most specimens grow no longer than 30 centimetres .
