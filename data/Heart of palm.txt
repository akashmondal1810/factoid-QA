heart of palm, also called palm heart, palmito, burglar's thigh, chonta, palm cabbage or swamp cabbage, is a vegetable harvested from the inner core and growing bud of certain palm trees (notably the coconut (cocos nucifera), palmito juí_ara (euterpe edulis), aí_aí_ palm (euterpe oleracea), sabal (sabal spp.) and pejibaye (bactris gasipaes) palms).
