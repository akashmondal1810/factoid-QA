the scientific name of broccoli is brassica oleracea var. italica. broccoli is an edible green plant in the cabbage family, whose large flowering head is used as a vegetable. the word broccoli comes from the italian plural of broccolo, which means "the flowering top of a cabbage", and is the diminutive form of brocco, meaning "small nail" or "sprout". broccoli is often boiled or steamed but may be eaten raw.
