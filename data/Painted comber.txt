the scientific name of painted comber is serranus scriba. the painted comber (serranus scriba) is a subtropical marine fish, classified in family serranidae, the groupers and sea basses. it is found in the eastern atlantic ocean, the mediterranean sea, and the black sea. confusingly, a synonym of this species is perca marina, but that name (as sebastes marinus) has incorrectly been used for a separate species, the rose fish.
