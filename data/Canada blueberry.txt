the scientific name of canada blueberry is vaccinium myrtilloides. canadian blueberry, vaccinium myrtilloides, is also called common blueberry, velvetleaf huckleberry, velvetleaf blueberry, and sourtop blueberry. it is a species of blueberry native to canada and the northeastern and northwestern united states, as well as the great lakes states. the fruit is a small sweet bright blue to dark blue berry.
