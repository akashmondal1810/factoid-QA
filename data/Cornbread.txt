cornbread is a generic name for any number of quick breads containing cornmeal and leavened by baking powder. cornbread is a popular item in soul food enjoyed by many people for its texture and scent. cornbread can be baked, fried or, rarely, steamed. steamed cornbreads are mushy, chewier and more like cornmeal pudding than what most consider to be traditional cornbread. cornbread can also be baked into corn cakes.
