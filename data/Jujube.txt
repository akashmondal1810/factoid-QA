the scientific name of jujube is ziziphus zizyphus. ziziphus jujuba (from greek ???????, zizyfon), commonly called jujube (sometimes jujuba), red date, chinese date, korean date, or indian date is a species of ziziphus in the buckthorn family (rhamnaceae), used primarily as a shade tree that also bears fruit.the fruit is an edible oval drupe 1.5?3 centimetres (0.59?1.18 in) deep; when immature it is smooth-green, with the consistency and taste of an apple, maturing brown to purplish-black and eventually wrinkled, looking like a small date. there is a single hard stone similar to an olive stone.
