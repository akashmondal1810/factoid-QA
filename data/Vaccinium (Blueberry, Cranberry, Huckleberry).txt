the scientific name of vaccinium (blueberry, cranberry, huckleberry) is vaccinium. vaccinium is a genus of shrubs or dwarf shrubs in the plant family ericaceae. the fruit of many species are eaten by humans and some are of commercial importance, including the cranberry, blueberry, bilberry or whortleberry, lingonberry or cowberry, and huckleberry. like many other ericaceous plants, they are generally restricted to acidic soils.
