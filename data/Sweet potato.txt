the scientific name of sweet potato is ipomoea batatas. the sweet potato (<i>ipomoea batatas</i>) is a dicotyledonous plant that belongs to the family convolvulaceae. its large, starchy, sweet-tasting, tuberous roots are a root vegetable. the young leaves and shoots are sometimes eaten as greens. the sweet potato is only distantly related to the potato (<i>solanum tuberosum</i>) and does not belong to the nightshade family, solanaceae, but both families belong to the same taxonomic order, the solanales.
