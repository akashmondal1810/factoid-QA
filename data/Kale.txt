the scientific name of kale is brassica napus var. pabularia. kale or borecole is a form of cabbage, with green or purple leaves, in which the central leaves do not form a head. it is considered to be closer to wild cabbage than most domesticated forms. the species brassica oleracea contains a wide array of vegetables, including broccoli, cauliflower, collard greens, and brussels sprouts. the cultivar group acephala also includes spring greens and collard greens, which are extremely similar genetically.
