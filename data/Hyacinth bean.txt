the scientific name of hyacinth bean is lablab purpureus. lablab purpureus, commonly known as the hyacinth bean, indian bean, calavance, seim, egyptian bean, njahi, bulay, bataw, a species of bean in the family fabaceae, is widespread as a food crop throughout the tropics, especially in africa, india, bangladesh, and indonesia. a traditional food plant in africa, this little-known vegetable in the west has potential to improve nutrition, boost food security, foster rural development and support sustainable landcare.
