the scientific name of mandarin orange (clementine, tangerine) is citrus reticulata. the mandarin orange, also known as the mandarin or mandarine, is a small citrus tree (citrus reticulata) with fruit resembling other oranges. mandarin oranges are usually eaten plain or in fruit salads. specifically reddish-orange mandarin cultivars can be marketed as tangerines, but this is not a botanical classification. when exporting began, local mandarin oranges were named after their port of origin.
