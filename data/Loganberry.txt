the scientific name of loganberry is rubus loganobaccus. the loganberry (rubus í loganobaccus) is an hexaploid hybrid produced from pollination of a plant of the octaploid blackberry 'aughinbaugh' by a diploid red raspberry (rubus idaeus). the plant and the fruit resemble the blackberry more than the raspberry, but the fruit color is a dark red, rather than black as in blackberries. loganberries are cultivated commercially and by gardeners.
