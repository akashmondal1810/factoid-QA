the scientific name of shea tree is vitellaria paradoxa. shea tree is a tree indigenous to africa, occurring in mali, cameroon, congo, cíçte d'ivoire, ghana, guinea, togo, nigeria, senegal, sudan, burkina faso and uganda. the shea fruit consists of a thin, tart, nutritious pulp that surrounds a relatively large, oil-rich seed from which shea butter is extracted. throughout africa, shea butter is used extensively for food and medicinal purposes, and is a major source of dietary fat. the fruit is edible and delicious. it tastes roughly like a fig.
