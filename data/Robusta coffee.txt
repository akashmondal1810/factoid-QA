the scientific name of robusta coffee is coffea canephora. robusta coffee (coffea canephora; syn. coffea robusta) is a variety of coffee, which has its origins in central and western sub-saharan africa. it is a species of flowering plant in the rubiaceae family. though widely known as coffea robusta, the plant is scientifically identified as coffea canephora, which has two main varieties - robusta and nganda.
