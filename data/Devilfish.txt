the scientific name of devilfish is myoxocephalus. <i>myoxocephalus</i> is a genus of fish in the sculpin family cottidae. most species live in marine waters, but there are also three freshwater species, including two that occupy northern lakes (<i>myoxocephalus quadricornis</i> and <i>m. thompsonii</i>). the name is derived from greek <i>myos</i> (muscle) and <i>kephale</i> (head).
